What makes a story worth covering (the "cracked" filter):

* someone actually built, tested, broke, or shipped a real thing
* concrete numbers: spans, loads, tolerances, costs, schedule days saved
* the gap between the demo and the dirt
* wild-but-true: the story a foreman would retell at lunch
* hands got dirty — lab-to-field, not slideware

Avoid:

* generic AI news with no builder angle
* press releases and funding announcements with no build story
* political ragebait
* engagement farming
* fake certainty
